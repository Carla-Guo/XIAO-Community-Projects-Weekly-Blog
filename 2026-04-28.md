## ESP32-Bus-Pirate by geo-tp

A **hardware hacking tool** with a web-based CLI that speaks virtually every protocol. Built on **XIAO ESP32-S3**, it supports I2C, SPI, UART, 1-Wire, JTAG, BLE, Wi-Fi, Sub-GHz, RFID and more through a serial terminal and web interface.

**Hardware Recommendation**
- **XIAO:** [Seeed Studio XIAO ESP32-S3](https://www.seeedstudio.com/XIAO-ESP32S3-p-5627.html)
- **Expansion:** [Grove Shield for Seeeduino XIAO](https://www.seeedstudio.com/Grove-Shield-for-Seeeduino-XIAO-p-4621.html)
- **Grove Ecosystem:** [Grove - I2C High Accuracy Temperature Sensor (MCP9808)](https://www.seeedstudio.com/Grove-I2C-High-Accuracy-Temperature-Sensor-MCP9808-p-3141.html)

[View project](https://github.com/geo-tp/ESP32-Bus-Pirate)

## Sky-Spy by colonelpanichacks

The official **OUI-SPY firmware** for **drone RemoteID detection and mapping**. Running on **XIAO ESP32-S3**, it passively scans WiFi and BLE for drones broadcasting OpenDroneID, extracting GPS position, altitude, speed, and pilot location in real time. Tracks up to 8 simultaneous drones with audio alerts.

**Hardware Recommendation**
- **XIAO:** [Seeed Studio XIAO ESP32-S3](https://www.seeedstudio.com/XIAO-ESP32S3-p-5627.html)
- **Expansion:** [Grove Shield for Seeeduino XIAO](https://www.seeedstudio.com/Grove-Shield-for-Seeeduino-XIAO-p-4621.html)
- **Grove Ecosystem:** [Grove - GPS (Air530)](https://www.seeedstudio.com/Grove-GPS-Air530-p-4584.html) ， [Grove - 6-Axis Accelerometer & Gyroscope (BMI088)](https://www.seeedstudio.com/Grove-6-Axis-Accelerometer-Gyroscope-BMI088-p-3157.html)

[View project](https://github.com/colonelpanichacks/Sky-Spy)
